# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Sehat Saathi is a community-driven project focused on accessibility, health awareness, and inclusivity.  
We are committed to providing a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for everyone who participates in this project — regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We value collaboration, empathy, and constructive feedback.

---

## Our Values

As contributors and maintainers of Sehat Saathi, we strive to:

- Be respectful, patient, and supportive of others
- Welcome contributors from diverse cultural, linguistic, and technical backgrounds
- Encourage learning, especially for first-time and student contributors
- Focus on improving the project rather than assigning blame
- Communicate clearly and kindly, even during disagreements

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Use inclusive and respectful language
- Be considerate of different opinions, experiences, and skill levels
- Provide constructive feedback and accept feedback gracefully
- Ask questions politely and help others when possible
- Respect project goals, scope, and contribution guidelines
- Keep discussions professional and relevant to the project

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
- Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation
- Dismissive or hostile responses to questions or feedback
- Sharing private or sensitive information without consent
- Any behavior that creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment

This includes behavior in issues, pull requests, discussions, commits, and any community spaces related to the project.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues and pull requests
- Code reviews and discussions
- Documentation contributions
- Community interactions related to Sehat Saathi

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior. This may include:

- Requesting changes in behavior
- Issuing warnings
- Temporarily or permanently restricting participation

The goal of enforcement is to protect the community and maintain a positive, collaborative environment.

---

## Final Note

Sehat Saathi exists to make health information more accessible and inclusive.  
Let’s ensure our community reflects those same values.

Thank you for being kind, respectful, and supportive ❤️
